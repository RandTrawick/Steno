# My cheat sheet

 * 13:00: #SBG, add BG to number for clock time
 * " (ending): KR-GS
 * " (leading): KW-GS
 * (add space): AFPS
 * /: OEU
 * ^ation: AEUGS
 * ^ian: KWRAPB
 * ^ion: KWROPB
 * {MODE:CAMEL}: K-BGS
 * {MODE:RESET}: KHRAO*ER
 * actually: TAOULT
 * basically: PHRAEUFBG
 * Capitalize Last Word: KA*PD
 * continue: T-PB
 * completely: KPHRELT
 * description: SKREUPGS
 * don't have: STKROEPBT
 * editing: EGTD
 * entire: SPWAOEUR
 * environment: SRAOEUPLT
 * except: KPEPT
 * exclude: SKHRAOUD
 * inform: TPH-F
